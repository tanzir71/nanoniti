Pipeline

Source → manifest → clean legal text → cited instruction rows.

Hybrid source collection

Starts from the Bangladesh Legal Acts baseline, then crawls the official Laws of Bangladesh portal for newer acts, amendments, SROs, rules, regulations, gazettes, and linked PDFs.

Auditable manifest

Every record keeps URL, title, jurisdiction, act and section IDs, retrieval time, SHA1, content type, OCR flag, completeness score, parent links, and relationship edges.

Parsing and cleanup

HTML is structured into acts, chapters, sections, headings, bodies, and clauses. Empty stubs, short records, boilerplate, and duplicate normalized bodies are removed.

PDF and OCR path

PDFs are extracted with PyMuPDF and pdfplumber, with pytesseract OCR fallback for image-only pages, including Bengali OCR when traineddata is available.

Model

Adapter-only LoRA/QLoRA weights for Qwen/Qwen3.5-9B.

Model card

Training profile

A100_FINISH_TODAY, max sequence length 384, batch size 1, gradient accumulation 16, max steps 300, non-reentrant gradient checkpointing, and adapter-only verified uploads.

QLoRA setup

4-bit NF4 quantization with LoRA rank 8, alpha 16, dropout 0.05, targeting Qwen attention and MLP projections: q, k, v, o, gate, up, and down.

Repair path

The current preferred adapter is repair-v2-citation-final-adapter, after targeted repair passes for citations, formatting, Bengali behavior, and context faithfulness.

Replicate it

A clear route for copying the process or changing it for another jurisdiction.

01

Collect and label sources

Swap in your own official portals, baselines, or court sources. Keep each source typed, hashed, timestamped, and linked to the original URL.

02

Build grounded SFT rows

Preserve the row schema, keep citations on every non-refusal answer, and add refusal rows for predictive, personal-advice, unsupported, or fabricated-law requests.

03

Train with persistence first

Run Colab or Modal with verified adapter checkpoints on Hugging Face and Google Drive. Treat Colab /content as temporary scratch, not as storage.

04

Benchmark before bragging

Test citation presence, refusal behavior, fabricated sections, disclaimer format, Bengali or local-language robustness, and faithfulness to supplied context.

Safety posture

Research support, never legal advice.

The useful part is the restraint.

Nanoniti is designed to cite official source URLs, refuse when evidence is missing, ask for clarification instead of inventing facts, and remind users to verify against the Laws of Bangladesh portal and consult a qualified Bangladeshi advocate.
